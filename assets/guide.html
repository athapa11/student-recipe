1. About

The app is aimed at students that want to explore cheap, easy and healthy recipes to cook.

Both phone and tablets are supported, including screen rotations.

2. How to navigate

To navigate through the app, click on the navigation bar icons at the bottom of the screen.

To log out of the account, press the back button.

3. Contents of the navigation bar

The home icon leads you to where you can explore new recipes.

The user guide which you are currently reading, shows new users how the app functions. You can share this user guide with the menu at the top right.

The bookmark icon shows you all the recipes you saved.

The settings icon is where you can change the theme of app to light or dark.

4. Navigating the home screen

To see how a dish is made, click on its image to show more details about the recipe.

Scroll horizontally to see all categories for the recipes available, and slide horizontally to view all recipes in a specific category.

5. Recipe details screen

You can save the recipe currently viewed, by pressing the save button.

When viewing the ingredients needed, you can press a button to direct you to Tesco online to quickly shop for ingredients.
